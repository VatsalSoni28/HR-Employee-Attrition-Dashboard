# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard built using the IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition dataset to analyze workforce trends and identify factors contributing to employee attrition.

## Project Overview

Employee attrition is a major challenge for organizations as it increases hiring costs and impacts productivity. This dashboard helps HR teams identify the main reasons behind employee turnover through interactive visualizations.

## Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends
- Identify departments with the highest attrition
- Study the impact of overtime on attrition
- Analyze salary slabs and employee turnover
- Explore job roles, age groups, and years at company
- Provide actionable HR insights

## Dataset

- IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- Total Records: 1470
- Features: 35+

## Key Insights

- Employees working overtime showed significantly higher attrition.
- Sales department recorded the highest employee turnover.
- Employees with lower monthly income experienced higher attrition.
- Most resignations occurred within the first five years.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives showed higher attrition than other roles.
